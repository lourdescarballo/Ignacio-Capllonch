FROM THE BEGGINING

I started taking karate lessons when I was 8 years old thanks to my grandmother. She said I had to dedicate my time to some sport. Then when I was 10 years old I started taekwondo classes. From then on my career was growing. Today, at 38 years old, I have disputed big fights and events around the world, in different modalities such as Muay Thai, K1 and Kick Boxing.
